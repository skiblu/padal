**Mala Shuddhi Process**
1. Needs to be performed at max a day before the Maladharna,
2. Place Mala on a Pooja Bowl ( used only for Pooja ).
3. Pour water , followed by Milk and Turmeric
4. Perform your daily pooja in front of your pooja room.
5. In the form of prayer invoke blessings from the elements of Air Water and Fire ( Deepam ) to help you embark the Mandala season.
6. Leave it there for a couple of hours or a day.
7. The same evening, or next day - clean it up and place it in front of your Idols or If Ayyappa Photo is setup by then
8. Show Aarati to the Mala. Seek Blessings from your Home God ( Family Deity ).
9. Pack it in a small Ziploc of Rice  . Please tag the Mala ( not the bag ) legibly with your full name .

Give it to your Guruswamy and let the divinity soak in !

`சுவாமியே சரணம் ஐயப்பா!`
